ENABLING OUR WORKFORCE
We live in a constantly connected, data-driven world. We have increasingly high expectations of technology and we expect a continuous stream of data, instant updates, and solutions that respond intuitively to our needs.

The BHP Technology team is relentlessly focused on improving the digital experience of our workforce, providing the right tools to enable our workforce to be safer and more productive.

As technology evolves, we will be at the forefront of moving from a digital experience to an ambient human experience within which our workforce has an unparalleled relationship with the connected world.
Mike Henry, Head of Digital Experience
